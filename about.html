About Covid-19 Fund Raise

Donate generously to the COVID-19 Relief Fund as there is an urgent need for funds to ensure the patients get proper care and the virus spread is curbed. Over the last month we have witnessed COVID-19 patients grappling with the scarcity for oxygen, essential medicines and even hospital beds. All the amount donated to the COVID-19 support Fund set up by Relief Fund will be directly transferred to the COVID-19 patients who are in dire need for funds to continue treatment. There are many cases coming where entire families are afflicted by the virus and quarantined at home, especially among daily wage workers. As a society, if we all put in concentrated efforts then we can manage this disease and try to control it till everyone in the country gets vaccinated.

  • We have started this COVID-19 non-profit service to combat one of history’s deadliest pandemics that is ticking off thousands of lives every day.Not everyone has been fortunate enough to stay comfortably at home in quarantine without worrying about their family’s needs. For such families we help them with monetary aid so that they can buy necessities to manage the viral illness during home quarantine such as thermometers and pulse oximeter. We receive calls for help for funds to get basic medication and healthy diet foods which are beyond their means.We support them with finding basic medication, set-up hospital camps with oxygen tanks and emergency care and aid to help them stay at home for the duration of quarantine.
